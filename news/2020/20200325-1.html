25/3/2020

全体教职员、家长及同学：

《延长停课事宜》

首相于2020年3月25日宣布延长《行动管制令》至2020年4月14日。为此，校方根据政府指示延长停课期限至2020年4月14日（星期二）。

敬请全体教职员、家长及同学垂注。

曼绒南华独立中学代校长

李才龙 启

To all teachers, staff, parents and students:

School Suspension

As the prime minister had declared to extent the of movement control order until 14/4/2020. Hence, the school is also suspended until 14/4/2020(Tuesday).

Please pay meticulous attention to this matter.

By Nan Hwa High School’s Acting Principal

Mr Lee Choy Long
