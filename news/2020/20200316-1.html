各位教职员、家长及同学：

2020-03-16

各位教职员、家长及同学：

《停课事宜》

为了防止新冠肺炎继续蔓延，首相慕由丁宣布落实全国行动管制令（Perintah Kawalan Pergerakan）。为此，本校将从2020年3月18日（星期三）至3月31日（星期二）停课。

2. 校方呼吁大家严谨遵循校方于2020年3月13日发出的《防范新冠肺炎事宜》通告的办法。同时，校方呼吁同学们在这段时期好好在家温习功课，为第一学期考试做好准备。

敬请全体师生和家长垂注。

曼绒南华独立中学代校长

李才龙 谨启

To: All teachers, staff, parents and students

School suspension

Prime Minister Tan Sri Muhyiddin Yassin has declared that the entire country will be on restricted movement order starting from March 18 to 31, 2020 to deal with the rise in Covid-19 cases. For this reason, our school will be suspended from 18/3/20 (Wednesday) to 31/3/20 (Tuesday).

2. The school encourages everyone to take note of the announcement related to the prevention of COVID-19 that we had announced on 13/3/2020. At the same time, we hope that students will spend time wisely by doing revision at home for the first semester examination.

All the teachers, parents and students please pay meticulous attention to this matter.

By Nan Hwa High School’s Acting Principal

Mr Lee Choy Long
